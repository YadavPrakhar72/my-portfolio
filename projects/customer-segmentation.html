Project Overview

The primary goal is to identify meaningful customer segments from the Mall_Customers dataset, which can help the business tailor its marketing efforts and improve customer satisfaction.

Key Findings & Insights

  1. The model successfully grouped the customers into 5 distinct segments, which were visualized using a scatter plot.
  2. The clusters can be interpreted as:
  3. High Income, Low Spending Score
  4. High Income, High Spending Score (Target Customers)
  5. Medium Income, Medium Spending Score
  6. Low Income, Low Spending Score
  7. Low Income, High Spending Score
